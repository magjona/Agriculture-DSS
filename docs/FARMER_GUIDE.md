## 1. Signing Up

To use the system, you first need to create an account:

1. Go to http://127.0.0.1:8000
2. Click **"Sign Up"**
3. Fill in the form:
   - **Username**: Can be your farm name or personal name
   - **Email** (optional): For account recovery
   - **Password**: At least 4 characters
   - **Farmer Type**:
     - **Smallholder Subsistence Farmer** - If you farm mainly for your family
     - **Commercial/Semi-commercial Farmer** - If you farm for profit
   - **Location**: Choose your Ugandan district/region
   - **Farm Name**: Unique name for your farm (can also be used to log in!)
   - **Farm Size**: In acres
   - **Soil Type**: Loamy, Sandy, or Clayey
   - **Water Source**: Rain, Well, Borehole, Irrigation
4. Click **"Sign Up"**

You will be automatically logged in after signing up!

---

## 2. Logging In

You can log in with EITHER your username OR your farm name:

1. Go to http://127.0.0.1:8000
2. Click **"Login"**
3. Enter:
   - **Username or Farm Name**: Your username OR your unique farm name
   - **Password**: Your password
4. Click **"Login"**

---

## 3. Adding a Farm

If you have more than one farm, you can add them:

1. From your dashboard, click **"Add Farm"**
2. Fill in farm details:
   - Farm Name (must be unique)
   - Farm Size (acres)
   - Soil Type
   - Water Source
3. Click **"Add Farm"**

---

## 4. Generating Recommendations

To get AI recommendations for your farm:

1. On your dashboard, find your farm in the list
2. Click **"Generate Recommendations"** for that farm
3. Wait a moment while the system analyzes your farm
4. You'll see recommendations appear below!

---

## 5. Understanding Recommendations

Each recommendation includes:

### 📊 Confidence Level
- **HIGH** (75-100%) - Excellent match!
- **MEDIUM** (50-74%) - Good fit

### 🌱 Crop/Livestock Details
- **Evidence**: Why this crop/livestock is recommended for you
- **Soil Analysis**: How well your soil matches the requirements
- **Weather Outlook**: Current conditions in your region
- **Market Insights**: Ugandan market demand and prices
- **Pest/Disease Management**: Local control methods
- **Planting Guide**: Step-by-step instructions

---

## 6. Editing Your Profile

To update your information:

1. Click **"Edit Profile"** in the menu
2. Update your phone number or location
3. Click **"Save Changes"**

---

## Tips for Best Results

1. **Be accurate** - Correct soil type and location make recommendations better!
2. **Check recommendations regularly** - Conditions change with seasons
3. **Use the planting guides** - Follow the step-by-step instructions
4. **Update your profile** - If your farm changes, update the information
